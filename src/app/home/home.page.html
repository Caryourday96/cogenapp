The system requirement is to evaluate the data from Siemens Co-Gen power plant and create meaningful results about the patterns in the data.

 Siemens Canada Siemens Canada is a supplier of steam and gas turbines used for power generation. Siemens has facilities across Canada including the co-generation power plant at Algonquin College. This is part of the collaboration between Siemens and the College. This plant uses natural gas to generate enough power to cover the baseline power requirements at the Woodroffe campus. Algonquin College The collaboration between Siemens and Algonquin creates opportunities for students to gain hands-on learning in the energy field and the data gathered from the plant can be used for analysis.
